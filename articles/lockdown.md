***

BendersMC has had a rough past few weeks with people getting scammed, textures glitching, and more. 

***



***

One of the few things that stand out are the new rank appearance, and last night, the removal of the colorcode permissions. Many players are outraged by those two things alone. Not to mention that we have had more things than this that have been happening. A few players in particular have created a temporary discord server to stage a riot. 

***



***

These two things are said by some to be bringing on BendersMC's:
> *“Downfall.”*

On top of this, a lot of players say:
> *“Ranks are degrading.”*

A lot of players have been thinking about quitting over the past few days after so much has changed. Over the past few months poverty has grown in BendersMC as the vindicator spawners have lost most of their value and don’t give much money anymore.
