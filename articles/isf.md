In Survival 1 there is a clan that many think is straight off of the line but is actually a new formation of a much older clan. This clan is called the ISF. For those who don't know, The abbreviation, "ISF" stands for The Imperial Syndicate Federation. 

A lot of players assume that this clan is new and nobody should blame them. But this clan was a pre-wipe clan. This clan’s old name was the Titan/Founder Army, then the Founder Republic.

Furthermore, CreeperKillerSP2 threatened to fire JackTheFounder_ (or back then Jackthetitan___). Jack chose to quit and soon the ISF grew in power.
