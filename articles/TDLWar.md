# TDL's Brief War
---

WRITTEN BY MR. MORALE

EDITED BY EULM AND AAYAN

***

Earlier today, TDL (The Determination Legion) declared war on TCE (The Celestial Empire) with no legitimate justification aside from their leader Xy's personal vendetta against TCE's leader BK. Xy had expressed hostility towards TCE ever since creating TDL as he was once a leader there, exiled for betraying BK's trust. TCE soon released a more formal announcement explaining how they wanted peace, offering to talk to TDL leadership and work something out, but also making it clear that they would respond in full force when provoked and sending out a call to arms to their allies and members in preparation for war.

TDL even mocked TCE for their offer of peace, making a public announcement declining it and stating that they "WILL NOT BACK DOWN!", escalating the situation and making their stance in the matter crystal clear.

However, TCE is a Sub-Imperium Faction of IOM (Imperium of Man), so IOM, along with some of TCE's other allies, responded by pledging full support to TCE, and the PvPers of multiple groups were quickly organised in a joint military force against TDL. On the other hand, TDL didn't gain the support of their only ally CE (Crimson Empire) as they recognised the threat of IOM and the fact that TDL were the aggressors, not TCE.

TDL's leadership had not expected this level of organisation and influence from TCE, and soon realised that they would not win the war, as a small group with few members, even fewer PvPers and a single ally that refused to support them. Xy reached out to a member of the High Council of TCE and surrendered, announcing to his members that he was calling off the war. After careful consideration, TCE decided to stick to their peaceful values and accepted TDL's surrender, announcing it more formally to display not only their peace but also their readiness to resume the war if TDL showed "the slightest hint of hostility".

It was a short-lived war, and you can be sure that TDL regretted starting it.
