# Return of the Guilds
---

WRITTEN BY BENI AND EULM

***

A year ago, clans/guilds made their debut in Survival, a system that stood apart from factions. Unlike factions, which focused on land and dominance, clans and guilds were built on unity, loyalty, and shared identity. Players felt genuine pride representing their group, but after only a few months, the system was removed due to internal controversies and balancing problems, leaving a void many still remembered.

Now, after a full year, the October Quality of Life Update finally brings guilds back, redesigned from the ground up with smarter mechanics and fairer systems. The new version introduces Guild Perks, a Leveling System, Guild Taxes, Suspensions, and even a Bidding System, turning leadership into both strategy and responsibility.

Guild Perks make every level meaningful. As guilds grow, they unlock benefits like reduced tax rates, extra member slots, XP and money boosts, faster cooldowns, cosmetic banners, and access to exclusive events or areas.

Guild Tax ensures stability — each guild must contribute a portion of their collective earnings to maintain their operations. Failure to pay taxes on time can result in penalties or temporary suspension, adding accountability to leadership.

Guild Suspensions act as a fair enforcement tool, targeting inactive or rule-breaking guilds. Suspended guilds lose access to perks and public visibility until issues are resolved, ensuring the system stays balanced and active.

With all-new features and improved management, this isn’t just a revival — it’s a new age of organized power, loyalty, and strategy. Guilds are officially back, stronger and smarter than ever.
