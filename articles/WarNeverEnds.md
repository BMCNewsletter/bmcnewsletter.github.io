# War Never Ends: UCSR Declares War on Imperial Empire
---

WRITTEN BY BENI

***

The UCSR has formally declared war on the Imperial Empire  in what its leadership has named The Phoenix War. The announcement comes shortly after a previous statement titled:

>“To the Defense of Liberty”

, where UCSR signaled its willingness to enter the conflict if no peace agreement was reached. 

In its first address, UCSR rallied its members and allies, including the Elemental Empire (EE), stating: “Our forces are ready. Our minds are focused. Our players are active. Our hearts, aligned.” The message described the situation as a struggle between liberty and authoritarianism, leaving open the chance of peace but warning that UCSR was prepared to act if necessary.

With no peace deal achieved, UCSR escalated by declaring The Phoenix War. Leaders framed this as both a rebirth of the group and a defense of democracy, stressing that while UCSR holds no hatred for the Imperials, it would not allow what it called an “attack on an innocent democracy” to go unanswered.
