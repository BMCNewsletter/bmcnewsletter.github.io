# A New Dawn
---

WRITTEN BY BENI AND EULM

***

The Holy Church of BendersMC has entered a new chapter marked by transition, renewal, and purpose. After years of dedicated leadership, Pope Grey has stepped down from his position to focus on his studies and real-life priorities. His departure, announced by Jack, led to a six-day conclave among the Cardinals to determine the Church’s next spiritual leader.
After nearly a week of reflection and deliberation, Atom was chosen as the new Pope. A long-time member of the Church, Atom’s journey began as a Pontifical Guard, later serving as Bishop of ACE and eventually as a Cardinal. His history of service, faith, and community-building made him a natural successor to Grey.
Former Pope Grey expressed his deep respect for Atom’s growth and dedication, noting his long-standing commitment to the Church and its mission. Atom’s election marks both continuity and change, a leader shaped by tradition but ready to guide the Church toward modern goals.
