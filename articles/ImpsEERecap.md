But this battle made the Crusaders realise that Emir was auto totem swapping. So the Crusaders call it shameful due to it essentially being a hack. The score was 10–1, and the Imperials lost by 9.

The final battle was an Imperial victory. But the Imperial traitor Sealio decided to attack the Imperial army. This results in Sealio getting dropped a few times. Then his friend Kracker also joined and was killed. So the score was 13–2, a Crusader loss caused by an Imperial traitor.

So this is all of the last 5 battles - an even loss and win streak. But who knows who will win the war? Only time can tell. It doesn't seem to be nearing the end and neither side is really winning.
