# EON
---

WRITTEN BY ISSAK

***

TNC has once again found itself drawn into conflict, as war was declared yesterday at 1:42 AM against the newly formed clan EON — the Establishment of Nubs. EON’s clan came under the leadership of Ben and Reflex, two former high-ranking members of TNC who broke away to form their own power. The immediate cause of the war lies in accusations that EON attempted to steal several core members from TNC, including Issak, Ice, Hihelloy and Iker. (names given by m2). The move was widely viewed as a deliberate attempt to weaken TNC’s internal structure and morale. 

In terms of military power, TNC currently enters this war with a decisive advantage in direct combat. Its frontline is composed of some of the server’s most practiced PvP fighters, including Nova, Zyn, Fluff, and others who have proven themselves in past conflicts. 

Yet EON holds a strength of its own, one that cannot be ignored. Their builders are among the most skilled on the server, with Moci and Ben himself recognized for their architectural talent. While this may not translate directly into combat success, it gives EON a great name. 

What began October 10/29  was not simply a battle, it was the opening of a story still being written, and only time will reveal which side will stand victorious when the last strike falls.
