##### SPEARS ADDED TO BENDERSMC
Spears are a new addition to BendersMC, alongside the other 1.21.11 features. They can be enchanted with a new enchantment with the name of "Lunge". Lunge can propel the player forward, give or take a couple blocks depending on the enchantment level which goes up to 3. Lunge however takes up hunger points - 1 per level of enchantment! This adds a new combat style with elytra and fireworks or bending moves. With this many players are now favoring spear and elytra in fights due to its unique and fast combat style. Along with this update Bedrock players are able to fight properly! In the 1.21.11 update we've received an abundance of new mobs such as the parched, and also received shelves! Shelves are blocks that can store 3 stacks of items, and if 3 are combined under the power of redstone the player can swap out their entire hotbar! Additionally, the server has become less laggy (Geyser must've updated).
