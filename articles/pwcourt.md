This business is called PortCity. PortCity is a city owned by two players; Jackthefounder_ and Blamster858. Pwarp Court is exactly what it is called. The pwarp is a Court for people to enact trials and sue people. This court is located in the courthouse/cityhall of pwarp PortCity.

This pwarp brings back nostalgia to many players who played Survival 1 before the first wipe of the two in a row. This playerwarp even has it’s own execution chamber! The owners of pwarp court are always safe with the execution and take screenshots of consents before executing. The people also are given a chance to empty their inventory before getting executed.
