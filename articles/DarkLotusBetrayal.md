# Dark Lotus Betrayal
---

WRITTEN BY REDGAMER

EDITED BY EULM

***

On 28 January 2026, the Dark Lotus Empire (DL)'s Discord server was nuked by a player called Xernex. Most of their previous 147 members were kicked, leaving them at 24 members. This gave Xernex even more suspicion for bombing their capital. Nuke (the leader of the White Lotus Empire / WL, DL's closest ally) said that Xernex “was in the White Lotus Empire” and later that he was: 
> *“an ex-White Lotus secret service agent responsible for researching terrorist and enemy clan attacks and infiltrating their ranks.”*

MoonLanderr (the leader of DL) then made an announcement: 
> *“Polar (a leader of the Elemental Empire / EE, DL & WL's parent clan) and I were investigating the recent attack on DL's Discord server. Xernex was given admin by Nuke according to the audit logs. He claimed Xernex was a traitor and ex-agent, but gave him admin. And then we looked deeper. And it all came out. The bombings, griefing, banning and framing. It was all Nuke. Nuke had permission trust in the DL capital, allowing him to enable TNT. After that he blew it up and put CE (Crimson Empire) signs everywhere, trying to frame Crimson (the now retired leader of CE). It made sense as Crimson held a personal grudge against DL. But then when we got too close to finding out, after CE proved their innocence, he framed Neon. But then he made a mistake - he also framed D3adforge (D3) and he and CE warred D3, trying so hard to gain DL's support in war to distract me. But I kept investigating until I proved D3 innocent. Then he made up a story about a group worshipping fake gods and griefing clans. I still didn't buy it, so he started panicking and tried to get rid of us by banning members. And now we're here. All evidence points out to Nuke."*

This severed all ties between DL and WL and led to WL being kicked out of EE as a state, after which WL became an Imperial state and begun forging alliances with various GDC states. Will this become a proxy war between the already fighting behemoths of the Imperials and EE?
