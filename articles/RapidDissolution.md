# Rapid Dissolution
---

WRITTEN BY MR. MORALE AND EULM

***

What was supposed to be a new beginning turned into one of the fastest collapses in BendersMC history. In a single day, The Legion, Starlight, The Colony, The Galactic Empire (but only briefly) and The Eternal Order (TEO) came together to form a new, united group — The Grand Union (TGU). For a brief moment, it felt like a new powerhouse had been born.

But peace didn’t last. Soon after the merge, TGU formed an alliance with the Elemental Empire involving military support and training from EE and support in politically neutral projects (e.g. building) in return. Although it did not associate TGU with EE politically speaking or involve them in their conflicts, due to miscommunication on both sides the Imperial Empire led the Global Defense Coalition (GDC) in a decision to declare war on the newly formed group. Though a truce was reached quickly, the decision left cracks in TGU’s leadership that only widened as the day went on.

Soon talk began about bringing The White Lotus Empire (WL) into TGU. Some leaders supported the idea,
but 140Cents, the leader of The Galactic Empire, was firmly against it due to past conflicts with Nuke, WL’s leader. The argument escalated, and 140Cents ended up stepping down and pulling his group out of TGU completely, scaring off WL from joining in the process.

The next day, internal arguments in TGU leadership unrelated to previous issues caused Rexonic to be removed from their High Council. This necessary decision had the unfortunate consequence of destabilising the group as a whole, and after frantic internal discussions all remaining leaders decided that the group must dissolve as a whole.

With it, major groups like TEO and The Legion disbanded too, marking the end of a potentially great merge that barely had the chance to begin. What started with hope ended in chaos, showing just how quickly simple disagreements can destroy even the strongest of merges.
