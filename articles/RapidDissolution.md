# Rapid Dissolution
---

WRITTEN BY MR. MORALE AND EULM

***

What was supposed to be a new beginning turned into one of the fastest collapses in BendersMC history. In a single day, The Legion, Starlight, The Colony, The Galactic Empire (but only briefly) and The Eternal Order (TEO) came together to form a new, united group — The Grand Union (TGU). For a brief moment, it felt like a new powerhouse had been born.
But peace didn’t last. Soon after the merge, TGU formed an alliance with the Elemental Empire involving military support and training from EE and support in politically neutral projects (e.g. building) in return. Although it did not associate TGU with EE politically speaking or involve them in their conflicts, due to miscommunication on both sides the Imperial Empire led the Global Defense Coalition (GDC) in a decision to declare war on the newly formed group. Though a truce was reached quickly, the decision left cracks in TGU’s leadership that only widened as the day went on.
