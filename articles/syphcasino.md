# Syph Casino

---

SyphCasino is one of the most well known casinos currently in Survival 2, with a vast variety of slot machines and a bar. Run by Syphmoonboy, a member of UCSR or the grand republic, SyphCasino is a simple yet pleasant business where one can gamble or drink with their friends. Though not necessarily the best in survival 2, it is certainly the most aesthetically pleasing with a floor design made up of Blackstone bricks and quartz blocks. It is divided into three sections;  The middle section, being the largest one with a large plinko machine as the centerpiece of the whole casino, the first section containing a vast array of slot machines, and the third section, containing a bar with some additional slots.



 If you want to earn money or just drink with friends, SyphCasino might be the best choice in Survival 2.
