# The Night Wolves
---

WRITTEN BY MR. MORALE

***

Big changes are happening. Earlier today, Bear, the Great King of Azure, announced that he will be stepping down as leader. After everything that’s happened, Bear shared his wish to build a peaceful village, far from the chaos and pressure he’s been facing lately.
But this isn’t the end of Azure’s story.
Before leaving, Bear passed the torch to HayotaSatan, who will now lead what was once Azure into a new era. With this leadership change comes a bold rebranding , Azure is now The Night Wolves (TNW). Hayota addressed the members, acknowledging how hard this moment is for many, but also calling on them to stand strong and help build something even greater.
In his message, Hayota made it clear this isn’t about forgetting the past, but about evolving. “I want all of your support. Let’s stay together, gather more people, and expand our territory,” he said. As a symbol of this new path, Hayota will also be switching from water bending to fire bending ,sending a message to those who doubted their strength. Despite the transformation, The Night Wolves will remain secret allies to Bear’s new kingdom, keeping their bond strong and unbroken. This moment marks not just a change in leadership, but a new identity for the group once known as Azure. Whether you're a longtime member or just watching from the sidelines, one thing is certain , The Night Wolves are ready to leave their mark.
