# TCU Conflict
---

WRITTEN BY MR. MORALE

***

Things are heating up on BendersMC as CookiesAndMilk, along with her team Zero Mercy (ZM), has started attacking the small but determined Continental Union (now The Eternal Order, TEO). The first attacks, around May 27th, hit TCU’s capital, Darrow, and Elemre, causing building destruction, griefing, and even some player deaths.
Attempts to resolve the situation peacefully failed, and CookiesAndMilk went ahead and declared war on TCU. According to TCU member Indisecio24, her goal is to push Twins, the leader of TCU, out of BendersMC entirely, targeting him and anyone connected to him. She’s even blocked communication on Discord, cutting off any chance of negotiation.
During the first attacks, a TCU member recorded CookiesAndMilk destroying parts of the city, which led to a temporary ban. But she came back, openly admitting to griefing and destruction, continuing her campaign until September 1st, when she officially surrendered after months of fighting.
