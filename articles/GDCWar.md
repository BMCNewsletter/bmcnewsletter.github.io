WRITTEN BY GOAT

EDITED BY EMPEROR

The Elemental Empire has been warring the Global Defence Coalition (GDC), primarily the Imperial Empire, for nearly a year now after being dragged into the conflict by UCSR as their ally and then left to fight alone as they dissolved. UCSR has since returned and rejoined the war, but they've been quite inactive and it's mainly EE's fight now. 

EE has recently been running an army training program to improve their PvPers (the Elemental Crusaders), and its first true test was shown as the Elemental Empire and its allies (UCSR, GK and TC) had a battle against the GDC. Both sides claimed there were very different results. EE and its allies all claimed that they won the battle by a significant margin, with scores of 30-19. The Imperials claimed the lead, with the score at 21-21. They provided proof, unlike EE, but proof of kills doesn't mean more kills didn't happen.

A few days later, Imperials made an announcement claiming that Elemental Empire was like “North Korea” in that EE has “brainwashed” their members to think that they are better than Imperials. Their evidence, simply search up “imp” in the EE Discord server. Soon EE responded with an announcement saying that if they were North Korea, why would they allow a lot of Imperials (Brody, Goat, Hihelloy, Sedlav, Ghust, and many more) to stay in their Discord server and talk freely, even saying “join imps” in their chats. EE also claims that Imperials have nuked their own state, providing overwhelming and undeniable proof. The Imperials then responded by saying that Cranix, the leader of the state authorized Emir to be able to nuke their Discord server after seeing its members begin to hold an anti-Imperial mindset. The Imperials also said the “incest kid” was a player by the name of Demxn and claimed that he was banned from BendersMC and nearly every server related to it for many reasons, but EE still harbors him because Demxn “constantly brainwashes” people there against Imperials.
