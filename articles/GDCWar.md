# War Against GDC
---

WRITTEN BY GOAT

EDITED BY EMPEROR

***

The Elemental Empire has been warring the Global Defence Coalition (GDC), primarily the Imperial Empire, for nearly a year now after being dragged into the conflict by UCSR as their ally and then left to fight alone as they dissolved. UCSR has since returned and rejoined the war, but they've been quite inactive and it's mainly EE's fight now. 
EE has recently been running an army training program to improve their PvPers (the Elemental Crusaders), and its first true test was shown as the Elemental Empire and its allies (UCSR, GK and TC) had a battle against the GDC. Both sides claimed there were very different results. EE and its allies all claimed that they won the battle by a significant margin, with scores of 30-19. The Imperials claimed the lead, with the score at 21-21. They provided proof, unlike EE, but proof of kills doesn't mean more kills didn't happen.
