# UCSR Happenings
---

WRITTEN BY LUNA

***

The Sixth Union of Communist Survival Republics (A.K.A. UCSR or The Sixth Union) has returned and is now back in full swing. Recently coming out of a crisis of inactivity led by the *Forest of Heroes Prerogative*, particularly, by The Spear of the Elements. Alongside Raiden was two others with the role of *Novemika*, a rework of the former name for Supreme Soviet of the UCSR “Novissima Supremem Soviets”. With the newfound stability of the UCSR, the Forest of Heroes Prerogative has ended, and the role of Novemika abolished. The three new Supreme Soviets of UCSR are sageplasma444 (A.K.A. Sage), thatguyraiden (A.K.A Raiden, formerly Lightning), and Aleutian (A.K.A. Aleu). With the two Novemikas Secretgent and Luna, and the former Supreme Soviet Syphmoonboy (A.K.A. Syph) now being the Konsulates. Various interviews are being held to fill roles within the UCSR, such as Executive Director, Supreme Justice, Minister of Economics, Minister of Construction, and many more. The UCSR has also gained multiple CSRs (A.K.A. Republics) of the Union, such as the Grand Tidal Empire, and the Death Empire. Blue Moon Incorporated CSR (BM CSR) has also obtained a new subsidiary (sub-company), The Builder’s Guild, led by Ash. This will boost both group’s activity, employee count, and total revenue. This will also likely boost the revenue RedsPotions, another subsidiary of Blue Moon Incorporated owned by Redgamer, due to its newfound activity.
