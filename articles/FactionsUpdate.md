# Factions Update
---

WRITTEN BY ASH AND FORWARD

EDITED BY EULM

***

The new, long-awaited Factions was finally released a few days ago, and a number of major clans like the Elemental Empire (EE), The Nova Corporation (TNC), the Imperium of Man (IOM) and Ragnarök seem to have already made plans there. Not only that, but other clans like D3adforge (D3), the Imperial Empire (Imps), The-Elf-Kingdom (TEK) and Skycity have expressed interest in expanding to Factions. All of these different clans having branches, plans, and/or interest in the new Factions server just goes to show how much hype there was and still is for the server, the efforts going into the project and the delays as a result being worth it in the long run.

The end fight happened only twenty minutes after the release of Factions and ended in The Goon Platoon (TGP, a group of the best PvPers killing, raiding and causing chaos across Factions without political ties to BendersMC clans) getting the dragon egg, although other clans like IOM succeeded in gathering the valuable elytras from the outer End islands.

Factions features new crafting interfaces such as the  “Component Forge” which allows you to craft materials for the weapons that you can craft in the “Weapon Forge”. There’s also the “Spirit Component Forge” which allows you to craft materials that you can use to craft items in the “Spirit Forge”. These Forges can be found at spawn as well as crates and the classic “Bending” NPC. Additionally, unlike Survival, the global shop has search functionality via the /shop search command as well as a different layout, and the server of course has support for factions via the classic “Factions” plugin with ranking, protected but raidable claims, a power system and more.

To summarise, the new Factions features a new forge system as well as unique UIs and further support for clans (or “factions”). Factions provides another format on BendersMC for players and clans alike show their creativity and play in their own unique way using the new systems and features.
