# October Clan News
---

WRITTEN BY BENI AND EULM

***

New beginnings in TEO
The two major kings of The Eternal Order (TEO) have officially resigned from the throne, leaving TEO's leadership in a new and volatile state. This means that alliances, pacts and treaties formed by the previous leadership will need to be reinstated as per their peace policies.
DL's war on the Imperials
The Dark Lotus (DL) have officially declared war on the Imperial Empire in BendersMC. According to them, this was due to repeated harassment of their members and players and will no longer be tolerated by the group as a whole. As DL readies their forces, the Imperials have remained unexpectedly quiet, dismissing them as a threat entirely.
We may soon see further developments in the war between EE & UCSR and the GDC as DL becomes more politically aligned towards the left, and TEO's new leadership is yet to be judged. October has brought a number of updates in the world of BendersMC clans, with only more to come.
