The past 10 days have had a wild update history. As we are moving into the Christmas season this year, we have already received additions this month that many older players have been wishing for. We have received some additions that were previously in BMC or had a similar feature removed, such as Chat Games and Guilds/Clans. We've gotten some new things added like 3 different spawn themes, custom plushies, custom plushie+, and PROXIMITY VOICE CHAT.
Guilds
As many have already seen, guilds have been re-added and are much different than the ones around 1 year ago that were removed. They have many perks and also a few “drawbacks” so that clans are stable and don't collapse. Those “drawbacks” are the guild taxes which make it so that every member costs +$5,000 plus a $10,000 base weekly tax to operate your guild. The starting price to create a clan is $50,000 and many guilds have been created already.
