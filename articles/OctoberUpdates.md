# October Updates
---

WRITTEN BY BENI & EULM

***

The month of October kicks off with major updates for the BendersMC community! First of all, the server has officially upgraded to Minecraft Java 1.21.9, bringing a variety of copper items and building blocks (starring copper golems and shelf blocks).

##### Introducing Furniture
Furniture has finally arrived in BendersMC! Players can now purchase unique furniture blocks from the Lotus Shop using lotus coins. These blocks aren’t just decorative — some of them come with special interactions that add more creativity, personality, and best of all, functionality to your builds.

##### Earning Lotus Coins
Lotus coins are an in-game currency you can earn simply by **playing, voting, and levelling up. You can also grab some directly from the shop at shop.bendersmc.co.

##### New seasonal sets
October also introduces two stunning new seasonal sets — the Dark Warden and Ender Dragon sets. Both are available starting from October 1st, offering a fresh new style for the players.

It's time to level up your builds and your look. October's update brings tons of new content for everyone in the BendersMC world.
