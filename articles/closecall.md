# Close Call
---

WRITTEN BY BENI AND EULM

***

The Nova Corporation (TNC), once one of the strongest and most respected clans in BMC, has managed to implode faster than anyone expected. In a matter of hours, what was once a powerhouse of coordination and strength turned into a symbol of chaos and collapse.

It all began when TNC unexpectedly declared war against its long-time ally, the Imperium of Men (IOM), claiming espionage and betrayal without presenting clear evidence. The move shocked both allies and outsiders alike, fracturing long-standing alliances and sparking outrage across the community.

As tensions rose, peace talks were proposed but Nova, owner of TNC, refused to engage in negotiations, pushing the clan further into isolation.

Then came the breaking point: Ben and Reflex, two of TNC’s key council members, were suddenly kicked from the leadership, accused of “putting EON above TNC.” The decision backfired instantly.

Within minutes, the clan’s most experienced fighters and organizers Fishy, Icee, Hihelloy, Bypass, Chrome, YJ, and many others walked out in protest.

In the chaos, Reflex founded a new clan named The Covenant, joined by the very people who once kept TNC alive. Even Almost, leader of the Imperials , weighed in:

> *“Both allies are dear to me, but I can’t let an ally oppress another ally. I’ve joined IOM’s efforts to stop TNC and defend what’s right.”*

Following this, TNC’s former state the Ant Colony publicly announced their departure, condemning TNC’s leadership and pledging allegiance to the Imperials.

Just minutes later, in a desperate attempt to stop the bleeding, TNC issued a 
final announcement:

> *"Effective immediately, TNC and IOM formally undeclare war. Both sides agree to cease hostilities and rebuild with respect and dialogue.
But by then, the damage was done. The trust was gone, the members had left, and the empire had already fallen.
TNC didn’t just lose a war, it waged one against itself. Because let’s face it ,TNC didn’t collapse… it speedran self-destruction."*
