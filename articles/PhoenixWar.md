# Phoenix War
---

WRITTEN BY yJ AND BENI

***

The first clash of the Phoenix War took place between the Imperials and the UCSR, now known as the Skirmish of Survival. Both sides have claimed victory, though results remain disputed due to connection issues and kill confirmations.
UCSR Report: 12 Imperial deaths vs 8 UCSR deaths. They stated that while often outnumbered, they managed to hold ground and even push Imperials on the defensive during key moments.
Imperial Report: Disputed UCSR’s numbers, arguing Nova’s repeated crashes invalidated several kills. They also noted multiple UCSR retreats and promised screenshots/clips to support their claim.
 Points of Contention: The main disputes center around Nova’s disconnect related deaths, a small number of crossfire kills acknowledged by both sides, and conflicting claims of battlefield superiority.
As of now, no decisive winner has been confirmed. Further evidence is expected to clarify results, but the Skirmish of Survival stands as the opening engagement of the Phoenix War.
