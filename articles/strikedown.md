Many players already know that the economy in s1 is currently under a depression. With the removal of Vindicator spawners and the downgrade of Sculk many player warps have been forced to shut down permanently. One of these playerwarps are Wardmoney. With the deflation spiral of sculk this warp in particular has been forced to shut down indefinitely. Some players have stated that this spiral was because of others abusing the farms and becoming a superpower out of it. Wardmoney, HolyEms, Emeralds, and more have been forced to close due to all of the economic distress. The economy of s1 will never be the same. This is agreed by almost every single player that we have met. Another thing they say
is “End the depression”.
