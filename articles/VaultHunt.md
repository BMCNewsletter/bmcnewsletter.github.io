# Vault Hunt
---

WRITTEN BY ISSAK

***

The Vault Event has been the talk of the server lately, with players racing to solve the five QR codes scattered around the world. Four go to hidden Vaults and one is tied to a brewing recipe. We can now confirm that the prize pool includes the full Elf Armor Set, and the brewing recipe itself is said to reward two Elytras, making it one of the more valuable finds in the entire event. With rewards this good, it’s no wonder why people are leaking the locations.
So far, Vault II and Vault IV have been completed bya group of players led by: Issak, GP, Almost, and Cedric, and the group they have put together, a mix of players from Imperials and several other independent groups. Vault IV’s riddle and location were leaked by D3ad and allegedly PaulieBeanie. Vault 2: Issak Xc, Vault 4: Issak GeneralAtlas
Two Vaults are still unsolved, and the brewing recipe remains completely undiscovered. With more players joining the hunt each day, the event is only getting more intense. Whether the remaining clues will be cracked by teamwork, luck, or another unexpected leak remains to be seen.
This event is supposed to last a long time, so there is no rush! So grab your friends and get hunting!
